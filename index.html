About Me

Greetings and Welcome to my personal webpage!

I'm Emmalie Cole, a dedicated computer science student at Southern New Hampshire University with a fervent passion for technology and software engineering. My journey with computers began at a young age and has grown into a robust skill set encompassing HTML, JavaScript, CSS, C++, Python, and Java.

This website, entirely designed by me, showcases my technical prowess. For an in-depth look at my projects, please visit the Projects tab above, which leads to my GitHub repo collection.

With over two decades of experience in video game testing, I have mastered the art of identifying and rectifying in-game glitches and exploits, ensuring a flawless user experience. My game-testing skills are a testament to my attention to detail and commitment to quality, pursued both professionally and on a volunteer basis.

While my primary focus is on my technical skills, I also have a creative side. I am currently writing a dark-high fantasy novel, and my writing passion extends to prose and lyrical composition.

I thrive in dynamic, collaborative environments and am enthusiastic about applying my skills to innovative technological solutions. My unique blend of technical expertise and creative insight makes me a versatile and valuable asset to any team.
