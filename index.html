About Me

Hi! I'm Emmalie Cole, a dedicated computer science student at Southern New Hampshire University (SNHU). I have a strong foundation in C++, Python, and Java, and my expertise includes debugging, testing, data structures, algorithms, and object-oriented programming.

With practical experience in alpha and beta testing video games since 2002, I have a keen eye for identifying and resolving in-game glitches and exploits. Beyond technical skills, I have a passion for writing and editing, working as a freelance songwriter and editor.

As an editor, I focus on content editing and ensuring clean translations from Russian to English, covering a range of topics from laser optics to stock market exchanges. I am also skilled in translating English-Russian and Russian-English texts, including technical subjects.

Passionate about leveraging my skills in dynamic and collaborative environments, I am always eager to take on new challenges and contribute to innovative projects.
